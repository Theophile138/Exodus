\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Information about the brightness and illuminated shape of a celestial body. 

Returned by the functions Astronomy\+\_\+\+Illumination and Astronomy\+\_\+\+Search\+Peak\+Magnitude to report the visual magnitude and illuminated fraction of a celestial body at a given date and time.
